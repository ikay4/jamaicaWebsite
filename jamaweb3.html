Attractions

Introduction

Now, when traveling to Jamaica, or any place really, it is always important that you know where you want to go! So, this page will list out famous destinations for travelers alike, and help you decide what destinations in Jamaica will make you have the most fun during your trip!

All sources/citations will be linked in the ABOUT tab.

Seven Mile Beach, Negril

Want to relax and play in Jamaica's most beautiful beach? With breathtaking stretches of white sand and the clear, aqua sea, the Seven Mile Beach, also known as the Negril Beach, is right for you!

Now, even though the nickname is "Seven Mile Beach", it is really closer to 4 miles, extending from Bloody Bay to Long Bay. It is one of the best beaches Jamaica has to offer, and it great for couples who want to play water sports and familys who want to relax! There are many activities you can do here, like play water sports or go snorkeling! Now, you will need to hawkers, which are people wanting to sell goods, but just a polite "No, thank you!" will work out fine! Also, resorts are all over this beach, so visiting this beach could also give you some thoughts about where you want to stay the next time you visit Jamaica!

Rainforest Adventures Mystic Mountain, Ocho Rios

After relaxing, it's time to get your heart pumping! Here, you can ride down the mountains on Bobsled Jamaica, which is a single-person roller coaster ride that rides you through the forests, or glide you through the trees on ziplines! You can also enjoy both the town and sea by going on the Sky Explorer, a chairlift ascending the mountains.

Besides those, they are many other attractions here, like nature walks, hummingbird gardens, ropes course, water slides and pool, and finally, a climing wall! So, if you ever have a thought about coming here, here is the location with the website attached!

The Bob Marley Museum, Kingston

Are you a reggae lover? Well, why not visit the most famous reggae music producer and fellow Jamaican, Bob Marley, Museum! Here, you'll see some of his most prized possesons, like his guitar, his favorite rooms, and treasured possessions which were donated by his wife! Also, there is a theater on-site, along with a record store and gift shop, selling all kind of Bob Marley merchendise you always wanted! Also, if you book a "One Love" tour, you can gain access to both the museum and the Tuff Gong Recording Studio!

Also, based in Montego Bay? Booking the Bob Marley Museum full-day tour will take care of all of the transportation for you, which also includes a real Jamaican lunch, so you can see and eat like the one and only, Bob Marly!

Zipline Adventure Tours

Now, finally, lets get your heart racing again with the Zipline Adventure Tours! If you love the nature and outdoors, then this is the best for you, as you get to kayak through rapid, zipline through the Jamaican forests, all while offering amazing sights of both the nature and wild life located in the forests! Also, there is no worry, as the tour gives all the safety equipment and a safety briefing so you know what to do in any situtaion and really enjoy your time exploring Jamaica's nature!

If you book the Jamaica Zipline and Kayak Adventure Tour, it will depart from hotels in Negirl and Montego Bay, and the tour includes hotel pickup and drop-off, along with lunch, equipment, a tour guide, and more. Remember, all persons who is participating in the tour needs to be of at least 6 years of age.
